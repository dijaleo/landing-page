This website is a tribute to the Omega Tribe band

Omega Tribe (オメガトライブ or Ωmega Tribe) was a Japanese soft rock and city pop project active from the early 1980s to the late 1990s.

Here are some members from the project:

...I wanted to give the group a name that was futuristically primitive, something with a kind of 'ying-yang' feel to it, and by that I mean I wanted to give the group a name that could help them lead the way into tomorrow without losing the richness of yestrerday...

-Kamasami Kong, KIKI radio station DJ
